Mirchi Bajji

Ingredients

  • green chilies
  • chopped onions
  • Oil
  • gram flour
  • turmeric,Red chilli powder
  • Oil
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. Slit the green chilies lengthwise, keeping them whole with the stems..
  2. In a bowl,chopped onions, red chili powder, and salt. Stuff each chili with this mixture..
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour,turmeric powder, salt. Gradually add water to make a thick, smooth batter without lumps..
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Remove the fried Mirchi Bajjis using a slotted spoon
  6. Serve Mirchi Bajji hot
